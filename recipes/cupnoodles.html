Cup Noodles

Description

a delicious and rustic oriental delicacy full of flavor and sodium. one cup is guaranteed to not be enough for one person- but two will be too much, creating a dilemma between staying hungry or overeating.

Ingredients

  • cup noodle brand of your choice
  • water

Steps

  1. open cup noodle package
  2. inside there will be a small seasoning pack. apply amount as desired to the dry noodles
  3. pour hot water up to the fill line
  4. cover container and wait 5 minutes
  5. eat

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

you can't go wrong with the grilled cheese sandwich and its simplicity. great for when you're in a hurry or making your kid's lunch.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • sliced american cheese

Steps

  1. preheat frying pan on stove with medium heat
  2. apply cooking oil of choice
  3. place two slices of bread onto hot pan and toast both sides for ~20 seconds each
  4. put cheese in between the two bread slices and flip the sandwich over after 30 seconds
  5. once cheese is melted, it is ready to be served

Hotdog

Description

a classic american staple found in all kinds of places like cookouts, parties, and even school lunches. easy to make and exceptionally satiating to one's soul.

Ingredients

  • hotdog bread
  • hotdog
  • ketchup and mustard (optional)

Steps

  1. cook hotdog
  2. place hotdog in bun
  3. enjoy
