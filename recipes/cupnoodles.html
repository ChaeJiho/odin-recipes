Cup Noodles

Description

a simple and rustic oriental delicacy full of flavor and sodium. one cup is guaranteed to not be enough for one person- but two will be too much, creating a dilemma between staying hungry or overeating.

Ingredients

  • cup noodle brand of your choice
  • water

Steps

  1. open cup noodle package
  2. inside there will be a small seasoning pack. apply amount as desired to the dry noodles
  3. pour hot water up to the fill line
  4. cover container and wait 5 minutes
  5. eat
